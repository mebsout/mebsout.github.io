About me

I am a postdoctoral research scholar since december 2014 at the University of Iowa. I am doing my research with Cesare Tinelli on the model checker Kind 2.

I did my PhD at the LRI in Université Paris-Sud under the direction of Sylvain Conchon and Fatiha Zaïdi.

My area of research is formal methods, and my interests lie in model checking and satisfiability modulo theories (SMT).

PhD thesis
